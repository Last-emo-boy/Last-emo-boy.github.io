A Story

2023/6/28 Miscellanies

Prologue One: The Lost Streets

On the streets at dusk, as night falls and the lights dim, a lost wanderer treads within this unfamiliar, bustling city. Towering buildings lining the streets cast long shadows, interweaving with the hurried stream of people on the ground. He threads his way alone through the crowd, with nameless faces rushing past him, as though an invisible barrier separates them from him.

His gaze is unfocused, bewildered and unsupported. He was once a pursuer of dreams, a young man with boiling passion. Yet, the relentless current of time gradually erodes his ideals, and he increasingly loses himself. Now, he feels helpless and lost, like a solitary bird that has strayed from its flock.

On the crowded streets, he pauses, gazing into the distance. The hustle and bustle of the city roars past, yet he feels discordantly detached. He can't help but reminisce about the past, the dreams and impulses that once quickened his heartbeat. However, the merciless years have faded those passions, as if they were treasures buried beneath dust and sand.

He stares at the unfamiliar faces, his mind teeming with thoughts. He progressively loses himself, like a forgotten leaf drifting silently in this bustling city.

Prologue Two: Seeking the Inner Voice

Just as he descends into despair, an elderly and wise sage appears at his side. The sage's silver-white hair and wrinkled face reveal the ravages of time, his eyes sparkle with a deep and lucid light.

The sage gazes at the lost wanderer, smiling, he says, "Young man, when you lose your way, seek the answer within. Amid the clamor, that faint yet resolute voice still exists, and it will lead you to a new direction."

The wanderer is moved by the sage's words. He closes his eyes, immersing himself in the silence of his heart. Amidst the noisy city, he strains to listen, attempting to capture those faint voices.

Gradually, he begins to sense a long-lost star of his soul deep within. That star twinkles with a faint but steadfast light, like a beacon in the dark, directing his path forward. At this moment, he realizes, his inner self still possesses endless courage and pursuit.

Prologue Three: Birth of a New Objective

When the lost wanderer opens his eyes again, they sparkle with a glint of brightness. He discovers a completely new objective, something that can ignite the flame of his soul.

The objective gradually takes shape deep within his heart, like a dazzling meteor. Despite the long and uncertain road ahead, his faith is unshakeable. He knows, this is his chance to rediscover himself, an objective that can make his life shine anew.

He decides to embark on this fresh journey, in pursuit of his own dreams. He takes a blank sheet of paper, sketching the blueprint of his future on it. He visualizes the possibilities ahead, delineating a new course for himself. Each stroke is filled with hope and expectation, each line represents the passion and yearning in his heart.

Prologue Four: The Journey in Pursuit of Dreams

The new objective kindles the fire in his heart, filling him with the energy to move forward. He emerges from the haze of confusion, devoting himself to the career he loves.

In the early morning, while the city still slumbers in dreams, he has already set off. Every day, he immerses himself in endless effort, pursuing the passion deep within his heart. He learns, practices, creates, continually surpassing himself, courageously facing difficulties and setbacks.

His strides are firm and graceful, his eyes flicker with an unyielding flame. With full-hearted commitment, with tenacious persistence, he continually strides toward his dreams. Every setback becomes a catalyst for his growth, every advancement strengthens his resolve.

Prologue Five: Reigniting the Beacon of Hope

He is no longer a lost wanderer, but a brave soul who has rediscovered his goal. His story spreads through the streets and alleys, becoming a shining sight in the city.

His perseverance and courage inspire those lost souls, showing them the possibility of reigniting hope. Although his name is not engraved on any monument, his story will forever circulate, he is a legend of the city.

His bravery will forever shimmer in people's hearts, his story will forever motivate lost wanderers, encouraging them to bravely face difficulties, seek the voice within, and reignite the beacon of hope.
